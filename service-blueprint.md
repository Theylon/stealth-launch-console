# Solo Founder CMO System

This is the canonical operating blueprint for the service. It defines the offer, the lifecycle, the package ladder, and the official mapping from skills to service phases.

## One-Paragraph Definition

`Solo Founder CMO System` is a productized fractional CMO service for post-product, pre-scale solo founders. It turns founder context into positioning, messaging, conversion assets, launch systems, and monetization improvements through a modular skill stack, while keeping every deliverable connected to one narrative spine.

## Best-Fit Customer

- solo founders with a live product and some customer or market signal
- founders across B2B and B2C
- teams without a strong in-house marketing leader
- founders who need strategy and execution structure before scaling

## B2B and B2C Model

This is one core offer with two explicit branches.

### Shared core

- founder intake
- positioning
- value proposition
- messaging system
- conversion assets
- launch plan
- monetization layer

### B2B variant emphasis

- ICP sharpness
- category framing
- buyer journey complexity
- proof, objections, and trust
- sales-assisted conversion

### B2C variant emphasis

- offer clarity
- emotional and identity messaging
- product-page and landing-page conversion
- referral and content loops
- faster purchase motion

## Package Ladder

| Package | Best Trigger | Included Phases | Primary Outputs | Handoff |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Positioning Reset | founder has a product but weak story | Intake, Phase 1, selected Phase 2 | wedge, positioning, value proposition, message architecture | Website and Conversion System |
| Website and Conversion System | site exists but messaging and conversion are weak | Phase 3 | website messaging, homepage, landing assets, FAQs, audit | Launch Kit |
| Launch Kit | founder is launching company, product, or feature | Phase 4 | GTM plan, emails, campaigns, content system, launch copy | Growth and Monetization System |
| Growth and Monetization System | founder has traction but weak packaging or loops | Phase 5 | pricing, referral, product conversion copy, optimization backlog | Ongoing Fractional CMO Layer |
| Ongoing Fractional CMO Layer | founder wants recurring senior marketing leverage | recurring highest-leverage phase or subphase | one major priority cycle at a time | repeats monthly |

## Package Definitions

### Positioning Reset

Founder trigger:
- “We have a product, but the story is fuzzy.”

Outputs:
- market context brief
- customer wedge
- positioning and value proposition
- message architecture

Out of scope:
- full channel execution
- ongoing campaign ops

Next step:
- Website and Conversion System

### Website and Conversion System

Founder trigger:
- “Traffic exists, but the site is weak or generic.”

Outputs:
- website messaging system
- homepage and landing assets
- FAQ system
- audit and optimization plan

Out of scope:
- full launch campaign

Next step:
- Launch Kit

### Launch Kit

Founder trigger:
- “We need to launch something coherently.”

Outputs:
- GTM plan
- email sequence
- campaign concepts
- content plan
- launch copy

Out of scope:
- pricing redesign unless explicitly added

Next step:
- Growth and Monetization System

### Growth and Monetization System

Founder trigger:
- “The story is stronger, but monetization and growth loops are weak.”

Outputs:
- pricing strategy
- referral program
- product conversion copy
- monetization backlog

Out of scope:
- full paid-media management

Next step:
- Ongoing Fractional CMO Layer

### Ongoing Fractional CMO Layer

Founder trigger:
- “We want the next highest-leverage marketing priority handled each month.”

Outputs:
- one priority diagnosis
- one major deliverable or decision package
- one review loop

Out of scope:
- high-volume production factory work

## Dogfood Rule

The service itself is the first client.

That means:
- the service positioning is created by the positioning skills
- the website messaging is created by the messaging skills
- the launch layer is created by the launch skills
- the pricing and packaging are created by the monetization skills

Every approved dogfood artifact must produce:
- one internal working document
- one public-proof narrative that can later be reused in marketing and sales
