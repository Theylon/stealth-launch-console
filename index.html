What the app produces
A structured “coming out of stealth” press release package.
  • market context brief
  • ICP decision and wedge
  • positioning core
  • recommended value proposition
  • headline options, quote bank, and distribution plan
What changed

The old page collapsed the landing page, the app, and the documentation into a single oversized screen. This version separates those responsibilities and keeps the interface compact enough to actually use.

Why it feels better

Less explanation, clearer handoff.

Instead of asking visitors to parse a giant mixed-purpose page, the landing surface now stays concise: what the product is, how it works, and where to go next.

Workflow

Five steps, one artifact.

1
Capture market context

Normalize the product, market, and announcement story.

2
Choose the ICP

Pick the buyer and pain moment the launch should anchor on.

3
Position the product

Frame the category, alternatives, and differentiated mechanism.

4
Define the value proposition

Translate the strategy into one believable buyer promise.

5
Create the press release

Export the actual launch document with messages, quotes, and distribution.

Built from tighter product UI patterns.

The redesign leans on cleaner system patterns rather than oversized landing-page theatrics. These were the main references for the refactor.

Text Input Patterns Used as the baseline for calmer form density, visible labels, and lighter field chrome. Textarea Patterns Used to keep long-answer inputs readable instead of turning every prompt into a giant block. Tabs / Step Navigation Patterns Used for the app stepper so progression reads like product flow, not a giant side rail.
