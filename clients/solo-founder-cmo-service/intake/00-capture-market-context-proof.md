# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The repo contained a large marketing skill library and a rough service idea, but the offer still read like a set of capabilities rather than one coherent founder product.

## What Changed

We turned the raw library into a single market context brief for the service itself, defining the buyer, the problem, the alternatives, the tone, and the open questions.

## Why It Mattered

This creates the strategic source of truth for every later deliverable. Without it, the service would drift between “AI agency,” “skill library,” and “fractional CMO.”

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

The service starts by doing on itself what it does for founders: turning scattered knowledge into one clear market brief.
