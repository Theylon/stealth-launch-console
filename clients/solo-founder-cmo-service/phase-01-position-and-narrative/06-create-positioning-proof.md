# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The service had a working position, but not yet a full positioning canvas that could guide the website, package ladder, and later sales material.

## What Changed

We turned the early positioning into a fuller market frame, comparison set, differentiation model, and website guidance.

## Why It Mattered

This is the difference between having a good line and having a usable go-to-market position. The website can now inherit a coherent category frame instead of improvising section by section.

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

The service does not sell vague marketing help. It sells a founder-specific fractional CMO system with clear alternatives, clear boundaries, and a clear reason to believe.
