# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The service had many capabilities, but not one compact promise.

## What Changed

We distilled the offer into a single customer-progress statement: this service turns founder knowledge into positioning, messaging, launch assets, and repeatable growth decisions.

## Why It Mattered

Founders buy movement, not lists of tasks. The value proposition now describes the actual transformation.

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

The service helps founders move from “I know my product” to “my market finally understands it too.”
