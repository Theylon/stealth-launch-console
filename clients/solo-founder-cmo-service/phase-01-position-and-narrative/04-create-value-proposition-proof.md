# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The service had a strong promise, but it still needed clearer ladders from “what it is” to “why a founder pays for it.”

## What Changed

We mapped the service from features to capabilities to founder benefits to business outcomes.

## Why It Mattered

That gives us a clean base for website hierarchy, package messaging, and sales conversations.

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

The system is valuable not because it has many skills, but because those skills turn into clearer decisions, faster shipping, and stronger conversion.
