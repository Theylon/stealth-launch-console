# Approved Deliverable

## Customer Progress Model

### Job

Build a market-ready story and execution system without prematurely building a full marketing organization.

### Triggers

- launches feel improvised
- website copy is generic or inconsistent
- the founder is the only person who can explain the product
- growth decisions feel reactive

### Pains

- unclear positioning
- fragmented copy and assets
- marketing work split across disconnected specialists
- no reusable strategy spine

### Desired Gains

- one coherent narrative
- faster shipping of high-quality assets
- better conversion and launch confidence
- a system the founder can keep using

## Value Proposition Options

### Option 1

Turn founder knowledge into a sharp market story and a reusable marketing operating system.

### Option 2

Give solo founders a fractional CMO layer that produces strategy and execution without a full marketing team.

### Option 3

Replace fragmented marketing help with one system for positioning, messaging, launch, and growth.

## Recommended Value Proposition

Give solo founders a fractional CMO system that turns founder knowledge into positioning, messaging, launch assets, and repeatable growth decisions without needing a full marketing team.

## Proof Requirements

- dogfood artifacts from this repo
- before-and-after service positioning proof
- founder-facing case studies once client work begins

## Rationale

This promise balances clarity, speed, and leverage. It avoids pure automation language while keeping the system advantage visible.
