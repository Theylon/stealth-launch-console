# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The service had a strong message direction, but the tone could still drift between strategic operator, AI workflow demo, and polished agency language.

## What Changed

We defined a usable voice guide with traits, tone ranges, vocabulary rules, and copy examples.

## Why It Mattered

Positioning is easy to dilute when the voice is loose. This gives the website and all future founder-facing assets a consistent editorial standard.

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

The service is not trying to sound louder than the market. It is trying to sound clearer, sharper, and more credible than the alternatives.
