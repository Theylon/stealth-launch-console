# Approved Deliverable

## Recommended Category Move

Choose a category carve-out rather than inventing a brand-new category from scratch.

### Recommended Category Name

Fractional CMO system for solo founders

## Why This Move Wins

- it stays close to language founders already understand
- it signals seniority without implying a full executive hire
- it gives the service a stronger frame than `AI marketing service`
- it leaves room for the system advantage without forcing the buyer to learn an artificial category first

## Category Point Of View

Solo founders do not need more disconnected marketing help. They need a founder-appropriate CMO layer that can translate product insight into one narrative spine and then carry that spine through website, launch, and growth decisions.

## Category Definition

A fractional CMO system for solo founders is a structured operating layer that combines positioning, messaging, conversion assets, launch planning, and monetization guidance in one documented workflow. It is lighter than building a full team and more coherent than buying one-off marketing outputs.

## Category Enemies

- patchwork freelancer stacks
- agency retainers disconnected from product truth
- premature executive hiring
- prompt-first AI usage with no sequencing or review discipline

## Buyer Shift Required

The buyer must move from thinking:

- `Who can make this one marketing asset for me?`

to:

- `Who can give me one coherent marketing operating layer for this stage of the company?`

## Owning The Category On The Website

- use the category line near the top of the page
- explain the stage fit clearly: post-product, pre-scale, founder-led
- show the workflow and artifacts as the proof of the category claim
- make packages feel like the applied form of the category, not separate offers

## Competitive Response Guidance

### If Compared To Agencies

Emphasize continuity, founder-stage fit, and the absence of bloated retainer logic.

### If Compared To Freelancers

Emphasize system ownership across outputs rather than skill depth in only one lane.

### If Compared To Generic AI Tools

Emphasize sequencing, review gates, judgment, and saved artifacts.

## Watchouts

- do not overplay category novelty
- do not stack too many labels in the hero
- keep the category in service of buying clarity, not thought-leadership theater
