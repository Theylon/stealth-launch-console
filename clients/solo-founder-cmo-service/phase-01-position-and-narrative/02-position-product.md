# Approved Deliverable

## Positioning Core

### Best-Fit Customer

Post-product, pre-scale solo founders who need both strategic clarity and execution leverage.

### Market / Category Frame

`Fractional CMO system for solo founders`

### Real Alternatives

- do-it-yourself founder marketing
- generic agency retainers
- isolated freelancers or contractors
- hiring a full-time CMO too early
- prompt-level AI usage without a strategic operating system

### Differentiated Value

The service does not just make assets. It turns founder context into one reusable narrative spine, then carries that spine through website, launch, and monetization work.

## Positioning Statement

For solo founders with a real product but a fragmented marketing story, this service is a fractional CMO system that turns founder knowledge into positioning, messaging, launch, and growth assets. Unlike agencies, freelancers, or disconnected AI tools, it keeps strategy and execution inside one documented operating model.

## Message Boundaries

Emphasize:

- senior marketing thinking
- productized structure
- narrative continuity
- execution leverage

Do not claim:

- full paid-media management
- broad agency-style creative production at scale
- magic autonomous growth

## Rationale

`Fractional CMO system` is stronger than `AI marketing service` because it signals seniority, operating structure, and founder partnership instead of automation novelty.

## Missing Assumptions

- how much the market will respond to `fractional CMO system` versus `CMO as a service`
- whether a narrower software-first category frame would convert better initially
