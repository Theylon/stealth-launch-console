# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The service could have been aimed at “founders” too broadly, which would have weakened the sales story immediately.

## What Changed

We chose a first wedge: post-product, pre-scale solo founders whose marketing is still trapped in their heads.

## Why It Mattered

That gives the offer a real buyer, a real pain point, and a clear line between best-fit clients and everyone else.

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

We do not serve all founders. We serve founders who have something real but need a clearer story and a stronger marketing system.
