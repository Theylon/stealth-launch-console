# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The offer risked sounding like either a generic agency or an AI toolkit.

## What Changed

We positioned it as a fractional CMO system for solo founders, with clear alternatives and clear boundaries.

## Why It Mattered

Founders do not buy categories they cannot place. This gives the service a believable frame and a sharper comparison set.

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

This is not “more AI marketing.” It is a structured fractional CMO layer for founders who are too early for a full marketing team.
