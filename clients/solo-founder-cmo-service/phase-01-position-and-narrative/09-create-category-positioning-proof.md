# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The service knew it needed a stronger market frame, but it was still unclear whether that meant inventing a new category or sharpening an existing one.

## What Changed

We chose a category carve-out: fractional CMO system for solo founders.

## Why It Mattered

This keeps the offer legible. Buyers can place it quickly, compare it correctly, and still see why it is different from the usual options.

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

The goal is not to force founders to learn a new category. It is to give them a better frame for buying the help they already know they need.
