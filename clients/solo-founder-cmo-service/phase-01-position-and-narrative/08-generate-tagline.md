# Approved Deliverable

## Brand Essence

- audience: solo founders with a live product and uneven marketing system
- differentiator: one narrative spine from positioning through launch and monetization
- emotional promise: relief, clarity, and momentum without premature team complexity

## Tagline Exploration

### Category-Led

- Fractional CMO system for solo founders.
- The founder-specific fractional CMO layer.
- Strategy, messaging, and growth for the company of one.

### Outcome-Led

- Build the marketing engine while you build the company.
- Turn founder knowledge into market-ready momentum.
- Go from founder-led story to repeatable growth.

### Tension-Led

- Stop stitching marketing together.
- Your story should not depend on your availability.
- Replace fragmented marketing help with one operating system.

### Operational

- One system for positioning, website, launch, and growth.
- The narrative spine behind every growth asset.
- From positioning reset to launch-ready system.

## Recommended System

### Master Brand Line

Build the marketing engine while you build the company.

### Supporting Category Line

Fractional CMO system for solo founders.

### Supporting Proof Line

Turn founder knowledge into a sharp market story, conversion assets, and repeatable growth decisions without hiring a full team.

## Usage Guidance

- use the master brand line in the homepage hero and deck cover
- use the category line in the nav, meta description, and package-intro sections
- use the proof line when the page needs more explanation than the hero can carry

## Rollout Notes

- test the master line against a more explicit service-led variant in sales conversations
- keep the category line close by so the line does not become too poetic or abstract
- avoid campaign-like lines on the main site until the core offer is more widely understood
