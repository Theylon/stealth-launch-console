# Public Proof Narrative

## Before

The service had strong message components, but no selected brand line to unify the homepage and future sales material.

## What Changed

We generated and scored tagline directions, then selected one master line and one supporting category line.

## Why It Mattered

A strong line gives the website a memorable entry point, but only if it still stays tied to the category frame. The selected pair does both.

## Reusable Sales Takeaway

The line is aspirational enough to be memorable and operational enough to stay believable: build the marketing engine while you build the company.
