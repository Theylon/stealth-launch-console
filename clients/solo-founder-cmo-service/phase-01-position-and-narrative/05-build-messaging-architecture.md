## Objection Handling

### “Why not just use AI prompts?”

Because prompts do not provide sequencing, review gates, package logic, or a narrative spine.

### “Why not hire a freelancer?”

Freelancers usually solve one narrow output. This service owns the marketing logic across outputs.

### “Why not wait to hire a full-time CMO?”

Most solo founders are too early for that cost and complexity, but still need senior structure now.
