# Luna Daily B2C Context Fixture

## Company

Luna Daily is a solo-founder-led consumer wellness brand selling a daily magnesium drink mix for better sleep and recovery.

## Product

- direct-to-consumer subscription and one-time purchase
- clean ingredient positioning
- sold online through a Shopify storefront
- strongest current traction comes from creator-led social traffic

## Buyer

- health-conscious adults aged 25 to 40
- especially people with sleep inconsistency, stress, or evening recovery needs

## Current Alternatives

- melatonin gummies
- sleep teas
- generic magnesium supplements
- “do nothing and hope sleep improves”

## Current Challenges

- the product story is emotionally weak
- the landing page is generic and ingredient-led
- the founder has early traction but weak repeatable conversion systems

## Likely Service Use

This fixture is the B2C branch test case for the Solo Founder CMO System:

- offer clarity
- landing-page conversion
- brand voice
- referral loops
- campaign and content angles
