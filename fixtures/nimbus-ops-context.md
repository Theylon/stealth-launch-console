# NimbusOps Context Fixture

## Company

NimbusOps is a B2B SaaS platform that automates employee onboarding, offboarding, and access change workflows for mid-market companies.

## Product

- Connects Okta, Google Workspace, Slack, GitHub, Jira, and common HRIS tools
- Replaces spreadsheet checklists, ad hoc IT tickets, and brittle internal scripts
- Includes approval flows, audit trails, and prebuilt workflow templates

## Current traction

- 34 paying customers
- Strongest traction with 300 to 2000 employee SaaS and fintech companies
- Average deal size is $18,000 ARR
- Sales-led motion with a 21-day pilot

## ICP hypotheses

1. IT and security leaders at mid-market SaaS companies
2. People operations teams trying to reduce onboarding delays
3. Fintech companies with heavy access-control requirements

## Buyer and user

- Economic buyer: Director of IT or Head of Security
- Daily user: IT admin or systems engineer
- Common blocker: security team skepticism about workflow automation

## Core job

When a person joins, changes roles, or leaves, the company needs every system updated quickly, consistently, and with an audit trail.

## Current alternatives

- Spreadsheet-driven checklists
- IT help desk tickets plus manual steps
- Internal scripts maintained by one admin
- BetterCloud for some accounts

## Claimed value

- Cut onboarding and offboarding admin time by 70 percent
- Reduce account-access mistakes during role changes
- Make audits faster because every action is logged

## Proof

- One customer reduced average onboarding completion time from 2 days to 3 hours
- Another customer cut quarterly access review prep from 3 weeks to 4 days
- Security reviewers like the approval logs and separation-of-duties controls

## Pricing and GTM

- Starts at $15,000 ARR
- Most customers buy after a pilot
- Primary CTA is to book a demo

## Tone and constraints

- Sound operational and credible, not futuristic
- Avoid claiming "zero risk" or "fully autonomous IT"
- Lean into auditability and reliability
