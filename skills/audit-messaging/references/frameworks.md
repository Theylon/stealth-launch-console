# Messaging Audit Frameworks

Use these lenses to evaluate messaging and recommend the correct level of fix.

## Obviously Awesome

- Check whether the copy reflects a real best-fit customer and real alternatives.
- Generic copy often hides a positioning problem.

## StoryBrand

- Check whether the customer problem, guide, plan, and outcome are easy to follow.
- Confusing copy often buries the plot.

## Breakthrough Advertising

- Check whether the claim matches the audience's awareness and sophistication level.
- Overclaiming often signals a mismatch between message and market readiness.

## Practical audit lenses

- Clarity
- Relevance to the ICP
- Differentiation
- Proof and credibility
- Objection handling
- CTA strength
- Message consistency across sections or emails
