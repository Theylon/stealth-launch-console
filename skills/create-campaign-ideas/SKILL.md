# Create Campaign Ideas

## Overview

Generate several differentiated campaign concepts and then expand the strongest one into an execution plan. Use this when the user needs campaign thinking that includes creative, channel, and operational detail.

Read [references/framework.md](references/framework.md) for the concept structure and comparison model.

## Workflow

1. Confirm the objective, audience, timing, budget, and brand constraints.
2. Create five distinct campaign concepts with real thematic separation.
3. Compare them on novelty, feasibility, brand fit, and viral potential.
4. Expand the recommended concept into a creative brief and launch plan.
5. Add channel detail, budget allocation, risks, and scaling scenarios.

## Output Contract

- Start directly with the campaign framework.
- Make each concept fully formed, not just a title.
- Compare concepts visibly before recommending one.
- Keep execution details tied to the chosen concept.

## Quality Bar

- Concepts should feel differentiated, not like minor variations.
- Feasibility should match the likely team and budget.
- Measurement must align with the stated objective.
