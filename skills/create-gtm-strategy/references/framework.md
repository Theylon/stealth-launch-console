# GTM Strategy Framework

Always include:

1. Market opportunity summary
2. Target audience definition
3. Value proposition and positioning
4. Pricing strategy
5. Channel strategy
6. Launch phases
7. Content and messaging plan
8. Sales enablement
9. Partnership and ecosystem
10. Metrics and success criteria
11. Risk mitigation
12. Budget allocation

## Guardrails

- No opening commentary.
- Keep the plan actionable.
- Use realistic timelines, targets, and budget logic.
- Reflect company stage and motion.
