# Messaging Architecture Frameworks

Use these frameworks to turn strategy into reusable message structure.

## StoryBrand

- The customer is the hero; the company is the guide.
- Clarify the problem, the plan, and the promised outcome.
- Remove unnecessary complexity from the core message.

## Made to Stick

Stress-test messages for:

- Simplicity
- Unexpectedness
- Concreteness
- Credibility
- Emotion
- Stories

For B2B SaaS, prioritize simplicity, concreteness, and credibility first.

## Practical message-house rules

- One core promise
- Three support pillars
- Proof attached to every pillar
- Objections handled before they surface in sales calls
