# Landing Page Copy Frameworks

Use these principles to write a focused conversion page.

## Breakthrough Advertising

- Calibrate the page to the audience's awareness level.
- The promise, mechanism, and proof should feel believable for that audience.
- More sophisticated markets need sharper differentiation and less inflated hype.

## The Adweek Copywriting Handbook

- Lead with a clear offer and a concrete benefit.
- Keep sentences economical.
- Treat the CTA as part of the argument, not as an afterthought.

## Practical rules

- Do not mix homepage goals into a campaign page.
- One CTA path is better than several competing asks.
- If the traffic source is known, echo the intent of the click.
