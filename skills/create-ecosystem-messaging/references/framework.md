# Ecosystem Messaging Framework

Always include:

1. Integration value proposition
2. Integration story arc
3. Use case messaging
4. Partner-type messaging templates
5. Audience-specific messaging
6. Social proof for integrations
7. Competitive positioning
8. Channel assets
9. Sales enablement
10. Launch sequence

## Guardrails

- No opening commentary.
- Emphasize joint value, not just your side.
- Use concrete workflows and outcomes.
- Keep templates easy to adapt.
