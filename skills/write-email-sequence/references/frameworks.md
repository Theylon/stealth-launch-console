# Email Sequence Frameworks

Use these ideas to write stage-aware B2B SaaS email sequences.

## The Adweek Copywriting Handbook

- Lead with a useful subject line and a clear reason to keep reading.
- Make the CTA explicit.
- Trim anything that does not advance the response goal.

## Made to Stick

- Keep the core idea simple.
- Use concrete details and proof to make the email believable.
- A small story or example can carry more weight than several abstract claims.

## Practical sequence rules

- Default to four emails when no length is specified.
- Escalate proof and urgency gradually.
- Match CTA strength to buyer readiness. Early sequences can ask for a guide or short reply; later sequences can ask for a demo.
