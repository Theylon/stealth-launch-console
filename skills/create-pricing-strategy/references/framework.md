# Pricing Strategy Framework

Always include:

1. Value metric analysis
2. Competitive pricing analysis
3. Tier structure design
4. Psychological pricing tactics
5. Freemium or trial strategy if applicable
6. Pricing page optimization
7. Discount strategy
8. Expansion revenue strategy
9. Price testing roadmap

## Guardrails

- No opening commentary.
- Show how the pricing model aligns with customer value.
- Keep discounting and expansion rules operational.
- Flag missing cost or benchmark inputs instead of inventing them.
