# ICP Sizing Framework

Always include:

1. ICP definition summary
2. Firmographic criteria
3. Technographic profile
4. Behavioral indicators
5. Organizational characteristics
6. Problem-solution fit
7. Market sizing
8. ICP tiers
9. Scoring model
10. ICP validation checklist
11. Expansion signals

## Guardrails

- No opening commentary.
- Quantify criteria where possible.
- Show calculation methodology for TAM, SAM, and SOM.
- Keep qualification logic usable by marketing and sales teams.
