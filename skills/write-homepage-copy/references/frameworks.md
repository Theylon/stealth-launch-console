# Homepage Copy Frameworks

Use these sources to write homepage copy that is clear, credible, and stage-aware.

## StoryBrand

- Clarify the customer's problem and desired outcome quickly.
- Position the company as the guide with a practical plan.
- Keep the page easy to scan.

## Made to Stick

- Favor concrete language over abstraction.
- Use proof and examples to increase credibility.
- Make the main promise easy to remember.

## Breakthrough Advertising

- Match the copy to market awareness and sophistication.
- Lead with the promise and mechanism the audience is ready to believe.
- Do not over-educate in the hero if the buyer mainly needs clarity and proof.
