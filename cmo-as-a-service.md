# CMO as a Service for Solo Founders

This is the founder-facing commercial packaging for the service.

## What It Is

`CMO as a Service for Solo Founders` is a productized fractional CMO system for founders who already have a product and some market signal, but do not yet have the positioning, messaging, website, launch, and monetization structure to scale confidently.

Instead of hiring a full-time CMO too early, stitching together freelancers, or relying on generic AI prompts, founders get one system that handles the strategic spine and the execution layer together.

## Core Promise

Turn founder knowledge into:

- a sharp market position
- clear narrative and messaging
- better website and conversion assets
- launch-ready campaigns and content
- stronger pricing, packaging, and referral loops

## Who It Is For

Best fit:

- solo founders
- post-product, pre-scale businesses
- B2B and B2C companies
- founders with some customer, revenue, or launch signal
- founders who need marketing leadership without building a team yet

Not a fit:

- idea-stage founders with no product or market signal
- companies that mainly need paid media buying at scale
- teams that already have strong internal PMM and growth leadership

## How The Service Works

The service follows one core lifecycle:

1. intake and context capture
2. position and narrative
3. customer and market validation
4. website and conversion system
5. launch and demand creation
6. monetization and growth loops

Every engagement starts with the founder’s current bottleneck, not a generic retainer menu.

Best for:

- market context brief
- customer wedge
- positioning
- value proposition
- message architecture

### Website and Conversion System

Outputs:

- landing page assets

### Launch Kit

Outputs:

- launch messaging
- email sequence

- content angles and plan

Typical result:

### Growth and Monetization System

- founders with traction who need stronger monetization and repeatable growth loops

Outputs:

- monetization optimization plan

Typical result:

### Ongoing Fractional CMO Layer

Best for:
- founders who want one senior marketing system working on the next highest-leverage priority every cycle

Outputs per cycle:
- one priority diagnosis

- one review loop

Typical result:

## Why This Is Different

Most founders end up choosing one of four bad options:

- do all the marketing themselves
- hire a generic agency
- buy fragmented specialist help
- use AI tools with no strategic system behind them

This service is different because it:

- starts with positioning, not random tactics
- keeps all assets tied to one narrative spine
- moves from strategy to execution without changing operators
- creates reusable documentation instead of one-off deliverables
- is being built and refined using the same system it sells
